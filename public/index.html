✧*̥˚ How we met *̥˚✧

1st of November 2022; The day we first met. Frieza the Product Marketing Manager, and Ryan the Cloud Engineer.

    During this first month we had already spent a substantial amount of time together due to the quite extensive month-long onboarding program at the office. Frieza thought I was an adorable nerd (honestly, spot on, so eyesight is definitely on point!🤓) Of course I was equally smitten and we hit it off almost immediately.

    So who made the first move? A couple of months of working together, I had convinced her to stay a bit too late at the Christmas party because drunk Ryan is just way too much fun and loves to dance 😅

    I may have felt at least partially responsible for the fact that she ended up missing the last train to Amsterdam, so naturally I called her an Uber home... all the way to Amsterdam 💰💸

    After repeatedly reassuring her she could consider this 1 hour Uber ride a christmas gift, I finally suggested a fair and mutually beneficial compromise in place of reimbursement; she could of course take me out for a nice dinner and we'd call it even!

    From there it's honestly a blur, we started talking more and more about anything and everything. Immediately inseparable. We both realised at this point that yes, we have indeed fallen in love with each other 🤭

TLDR: Only a few months passed before I realised it was clear that I would propose, and I had a nice window of opportunity to do it rather soon. So with the help from my best friend Olin (who happened to be visiting me from Canada at the time), and in the span of literally a couple of days, I chose a romantic destination - Bruges - and on 30 April 2023, I proposed in what honestly turned out to be a perfect storybook proposal. I'm sure I'll tell you all about it in March.

Frieza: Ryan ate and left no crumbs on the whole proposal trip - it was an easy YES! 💍


And now here we are, nearly a year later (or more, depending on when you're reading this); We are fully and completely inseparable and so unbelievably excited to start on this journey together.

We can't wait to celebrate our love and party with you soon! 🕺🏻💃🪩
